    About Us

    We are a family-owned, home-based business located in Winnipeg, Manitoba.

    Hi! We are Fernand and Ellen from the Valley Gardens neighbourhood.

    • We own a professional Mobile Auto Detailing business.
    • We are consistent, flexible, and efficient.

    For our mobile auto detailing services, we offer one of the best! Our services are offered in a package deal for full, exterior, or interior detailing. We also offer add-on services like headlight restorations, under carriage washes, engine bay detailing, and more.

    We mostly use eco-friendly, bio-degradable, and non-toxic products in our interior and exterior detailing services! This will keep your car looking brand new, clean, fresh, and maintanable, without the toxic smell of chemicals.

    *NOTE*
    This is a family-owned, home-based business and we only do detailing in your home locations or in our home garage. Once we arrive at your house, please let us know where your water spigot and electrical outlets are located and we can start the job!
